About Punjab Educare

Empowering students through accessible learning solutions.

Punjab Educare is an initiative by the Department of School Education, Punjab, designed to promote digital learning.

Our mission is to make quality education available to every learner through innovative online resources.

Our Mission

To provide free, high-quality educational resources to every student and teacher across Punjab through digital learning.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, technology-driven education ecosystem that bridges the gap between students and quality content.

Our Core Objectives

Enhance digital literacy in schools
Empower teachers with modern tools
Encourage self-learning and innovation
Ensure education accessibility for all
